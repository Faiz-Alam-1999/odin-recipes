Cake

Description

Cake is a delectable dessert enjoyed worldwide, known for its soft, fluffy texture and sweet taste. Typically made from a mixture of flour, sugar, eggs, and butter, cakes come in a myriad of flavors, ranging from classic vanilla and chocolate to exotic combinations like red velvet and lemon chiffon. Often adorned with frosting, icing, or fruit toppings, cakes are a centerpiece of celebrations, from birthdays to weddings, and serve as a symbol of indulgence and joy. Whether served as a simple sheet cake or elaborately decorated masterpiece, cake delights taste buds and brings people together to savor its irresistible sweetness.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter (or oil)
  • Baking powder (or baking soda)
  • Milk (or water)
  • Vanilla extract (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven and prepare pans.
  2. Mix dry ingredients.
  3. Mix wet ingredients.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  5. Pour batter into pans and bake.
  6. Cool cakes before serving or frosting.
