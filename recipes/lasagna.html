Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide flat pasta noodles with various ingredients such as seasoned ground meat, tomato sauce, creamy b√©chamel sauce, and cheese. The layers are typically repeated several times to create a hearty and flavorful dish. It's then baked until the noodles are tender and the cheese is melted and bubbly, resulting in a deliciously satisfying meal that's perfect for gatherings or cozy family dinners. Lasagna is known for its rich and comforting taste, making it a beloved favorite in many households around the world.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground meat (usually beef or Italian sausage)
  • Tomato sauce or marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook noodles.
  2. Brown meat with onion and garlic; add sauce.
  3. Mix ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  4. Layer sauce, noodles, cheese mixture.
  5. Repeat layers, ending with cheese.
  6. Bake covered, then uncovered.
  7. Let cool slightly before serving. Enjoy!
